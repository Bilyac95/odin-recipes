Milk Steak

Main page

Description

This is a recipe for the widely loved milk steak. Best enjoyed with a glass of fight milk made with fresh crow's eggs.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb tough cube steak, roughly 1 inch thick
  • 1 quart whole fat milk
  • 1/4 lb salted butter
  • handfull of jelly-beans (coloring optional)

Steps

  1. Bring your hotplate to maximum temperature
  2. In a teflon-pan, add butter and allow to melt until fully brown
  3. Add steak to pan and cook until desired texture
  4. Add milk and bring to boil. Boil for around 3 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and allow steak to rest. Garnish with your favorite jelly beans.
