Fight Milk

Main page

Description

This is a recipe for fight milk, the official sponsor of the UFC.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of whole fat milk (preferably unpasteurized, as recommended by RFK Jr)
  • 3-6 crow's eggs
  • 1 quart of vodka (I used Explorer Vodka but any low grain alcohol will work)

Steps

  1. Crack crow's eggs, including shells, into your milk container.
  2. Shake container violently.
  3. Pour vodka and repeat violent shaking.
  4. Enjoy in a pint glass or directly from container.
  5. Pick a fight with the closest Jabroni you can find.
